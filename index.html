MY PRECIOUS EXPERIENCE

Police cadet, March 3, 2018

An unforgettable experience is all that has to do with police cadets because my height is not enough as stated in the PDRM's requirement even though I have a very deep interest in this field. People with strong souls can lift a rifle, aim right and shoot right at the target because every time a bullet is released from the rifle, the kick (recoil) to the back is really strong, especially if you use an M16. People who are just learning to shoot really have shoulder pain every time after the end of a shooting training session.
